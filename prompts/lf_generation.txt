### Response Format

List all new criteria in the response. For each, include:

- `criterion`: a short name for the criterion  
- `description`: an explanation that can guide an LLM to identify matching texts. This should be very specific and clear, 1-3 sentences.
- `class`: the class to which the criterion applies. It ALWAYS must be {{ label_name }}

The description should consist of several sentences and explain the types of texts that match this criterion. Descriptions may contain keywords or concepts characteristic of the texts or the general theme of the texts.
